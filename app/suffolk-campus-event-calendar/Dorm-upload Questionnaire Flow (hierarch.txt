Dorm-upload Questionnaire Flow (hierarchical)
============================================

1. Dorm / Building / Residence Hall name
2. Room number
3. Campus name
4. Floor
5. Academic year lived there
6. Beds in the room

7. What type of dorm room do you live in?
    ├── Suite → continue at 7.1
    ├── Apartment → continue at 7.1
    ├── Adjoining rooms → continue at 7.1
    └── Other → continue at 7.1
        7.1  Dorm room occupants count

        7.3  Private bathroom?
            ├── No  → continue at 7.4
            └── Yes
                •  Upload private-bathroom photos
                •  Upload private-bathroom videos

        7.4  Private common space / living room?
            ├── No  → continue at 7.5
            └── Yes
                7.4.1  Does your common space have a different room number?
                    ├── No
                    └── Yes
                        •  Upload private-common-space photos
                        •  Upload private-common-space videos

        7.5  Private kitchen?
            ├── No  → proceed to step 11 (public facilities)
            └── Yes
                •  Upload private-kitchen photos
                •  Upload private-kitchen videos

11. Public bathroom?
    ├── No  → step 12
    └── Yes
        •  Upload public-bathroom photos
        •  Upload public-bathroom videos

12. Public common space?
    ├── No  → step 13
    └── Yes
        •  Upload public-common-space photos
        •  Upload public-common-space videos

13. Public kitchen?
    ├── No  → step 14
    └── Yes
        •  Select public-kitchen count (one / more than one)
        •  Specify floors for kitchens (repeatable)
        •  Upload public-kitchen photos
        •  Upload public-kitchen videos

14. Public game room?
    ├── No  → step 15
    └── Yes
        •  Select public-game-room count (one / more than one)
        •  If more than one: exact count
        •  Specify floors for game rooms (repeatable)
        •  Upload public-game-room photos
        •  Upload public-game-room videos

15. Other dorm photos to upload?
    ├── No  → step 16
    └── Yes
        •  Upload additional dorm-room photos
        •  Upload additional dorm-room videos
        •  (Optional) Plaque photo

16. Amenities (free-text list)
17. Comments (free text)
18. Student email (verification)
19. Submit
